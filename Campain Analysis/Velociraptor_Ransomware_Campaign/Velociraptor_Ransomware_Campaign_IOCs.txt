# Velociraptor Ransomware Campaign IOCs
# Source: Cisco Talos - Velociraptor leveraged in ransomware attacks
# Date: October 6, 2025
# Campaign: Advanced threat actors leveraging Velociraptor DFIR platform for ransomware operations

## Threat Actor Profile

### Primary Identifiers
- **Campaign Name**: Velociraptor Ransomware Campaign
- **Threat Type**: Ransomware with DFIR tool abuse
- **Associated Ransomware**: LockBit, Babuk Ransomware
- **Threat Group**: AEGIS
- **Threat Level**: High
- **Source**: Cisco Talos Intelligence

### Campaign Overview
- **Attack Vector**: Abuse of legitimate DFIR tools
- **Primary Objective**: Data exfiltration, lateral movement, and ransomware deployment
- **Target**: Organizations with Velociraptor deployments
- **Infection Chain**: Velociraptor abuse → Data collection → Lateral movement → Ransomware deployment
